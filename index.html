Privacy Policy

Smart Gallery: Photo Cleaner

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Smart Gallery: Photo Cleaner ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Smart Gallery: Photo Cleaner does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All photo processing is performed entirely on your device.

  • We do not access your photos beyond what is necessary to display and organize them within the app.
  • We do not upload your photos or any other data to external servers.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information.

2. Photo Library Access

The app requires access to your photo library to detect duplicates and help you clean your gallery. This access is used solely within the app and no photos leave your device.

3. Purchases & Subscriptions

Smart Gallery offers an optional Premium yearly subscription. All purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Purchase history is managed by Apple and RevenueCat, which processes subscription status on our behalf.

RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data (e.g. subscription status, purchase date) to manage entitlements. No personally identifiable information is shared.

You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at: revenuecat.com/privacy

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat – subscription management. Collects anonymous purchase data only.
  • Apple App Store – payment processing and app distribution.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used in this app.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
